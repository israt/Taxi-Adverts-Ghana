What You Get

With TAGh media, you are assured of a campaign that is:

High Impact:

Double sided, LED lit advertisements which are eye-level, attention-grabbing and prominent. Our illuminated taxi-top ads ensure that you can saturate a market 24 hours a day, seven days a week, all month long.

High Frequency:

Large fleets of taxis shuttle between the major airports, hotels, businesses, residential areas and shopping centers. The rate of recurrence of the taxi-top ads creates optimal brand recollection and is sure to capture share of mind for your brand.

Maximum Exposure:

Outdoor advertisements typically provide the highest amount of reach. With our taxis, we give your brand extensive exposure and reach with either a mass execution or highly targeted approach depending on your needs.

Flexible:

Taxi-Top advertising can be for short-term direct response initiatives or lengthier brand awareness campaigns. We tailor bespoke ads that fulfill your objectives and goals because we understand that no two clients are the same.
