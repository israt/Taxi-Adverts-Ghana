About Us

TAGh Media was established in 2009 with a clear vision – to revolutionize the advertising landscape in Ghana by providing dynamic, out of the box media solutions. This was to be achieved by spearheading the adoption of ‘Taxi-Top’ advertising in Ghana, a tried and tested concept across the globe which continues to be utilized successfully. Taxi tops advertising is a cost-efficient, high impact medium, easily deployable in highly populated urban centers for local, national and international businesses.

Taxi top advertising offers visual mobility: an element to advertising that no other media company currently offers in Ghana. TAGh Media operations are nationwide but are centralized in Accra, where we deliver meticulously designed original and striking Taxi-Top ads.

Our mission is to provide our clients with exceptional brand presence and exposure through targeted campaigns and ensure that your brand enjoys increased sales and enhanced customer satisfaction.

First to market: We have tried and tested and as such we know what we are doing when we design and execute a campaign.
