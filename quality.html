Quality Assurance

Your advertising is ultimately a reflection of your companyâ€™s image, a fact we focus on from our first meeting through to final campaign deployment. The checks and balances we implement to assure high quality and professionalism include:

Database:

  • We have built a proprietary database, suited for the Ghanaian market, which affords us full control of all aspects of our taxi fleets.

Taxi Fleet:

  • One of our requirements for our drivers is a roadworthiness certificate. We keep track of the renewal dates in our customized TAGh media database and ensure all our drivers are road worthy before they are included in any campaign. This ensures that all our vehicles are in great condition.
  • A visual inspection is performed before every campaign to ensure every vehicle employed is in good physical condition and free of any damage. This helps portray an immaculate and sharp image for every campaign.
  • Random weekly taxi-rank visits at crucial times will be performed to ensure drivers involved in any campaign are fully operational.

Taxi-Top:

  • Monthly inspection of taxi-tops and LED lighting to ensure great condition of the taxi-tops and full visibility of campaigns throughout the day and night.
  • Semi-Annual LED replacement to ensure that every ad maintains the same level of brightness throughout the duration of the campaign.
